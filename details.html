Details

The renovated, traditional style cottage sits in a secure garden with a private, gated parking area.
In total the cottage sleeps six people and has space to park three cars comfortably.

Downstairs

Features

    There is a fully fitted kitchen with a dining area. The kitchen is equipped with a fridge, freezer, oven, hob, microwave oven, dishwasher, and washing machine and tumble dryer can be found in the utility room.
    There is also a spacious living room with a large window at the rear and sliding doors at the front, leading to a patio decking and picnic bench.
    To complete the downstairs, there is a double bedroom and family bathroom with a bath, shower, WC, sink and underfloor heating.
    There is also a utility room with a washing machine, tumble dryer and sink, and space to store dirty gear after a day of exploring.

Upstairs

    Upstairs there are two twin bedrooms.
