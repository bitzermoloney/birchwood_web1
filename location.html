Location

Travelling along the A87 from either Inverness or Fort William, turn right just past Eilean Donan Castle into the village of Dornie. Follow the road round to the right, past the old Dornie Stores, and continue beside the loch for about 1 mile. Birchwood is situated on the right hand side in the area of Bundalloch.
If travelling from Kyle turn left into Dornie directly over the bridge and continue as above.
