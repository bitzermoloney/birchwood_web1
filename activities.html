Local Activities

Eilean Donan Castle

Explore the iconic Eilean Donan Castle, a historic stronghold on a small island. Take a short boat trip or walk across the causeway to visit this picturesque landmark, perfect for history enthusiasts and photographers.

Hiking in the Highlands

Enjoy scenic hikes through the stunning Scottish Highlands. From gentle walks along Loch Long to more challenging trails, discover breathtaking landscapes, wildlife, and fresh mountain air.

Boat Trips on Loch Long

Embark on a relaxing boat trip across Loch Long. Spot seals, birds, and enjoy the serene waters. Ideal for fishing, kayaking, or simply unwinding with nature.

Village Exploration in Dornie

Wander through the charming village of Dornie. Visit local shops, enjoy a meal at the pub, or stroll along the waterfront. A great way to experience authentic Highland life.
